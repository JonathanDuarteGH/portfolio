About Jonathan

Hello there! My name is Jonathan and I'm a graduate from Assumption College and the University of Notre Dame.

I started my journey as a engineer at a very young age by assembling a miniature sized model kit called a Zoid. I was amazed on how each modular part was pieced together with the main power unit. I then wondered if there were any physical implications to assemble this model in reality.

That is, what were the newtonian mechanics used to drive out this model and how each piece was developed using current technology.

The curiosity I had for machines naturally became an interest in mechatronics, automation and especially coding.

During my free time, I enjoy learning all kinds of new software, programming, and physical assembly skills in order to build new and challenging projects.

Some of my favorite things to do include:

  • Fixing unresponsive computers
  • Contributing to open source websites
  • Learning modern standards and best practices of industries
